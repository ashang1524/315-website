    Technical Areas of Interest
    On a broad level, my interest in coding stems from my exploration in other fields, which led me to find coding, where I can apply one core approach to improve every aspect of my life. On a deeper level, I realized my hobbies all share a common trait with coding: flexibility. While running requires planning the trip and tennis requires you to have a game plan before stepping foot onto the court, they're both very flexible and random. There are numerous ways to run a mile or play a tennis match. Rather than having one approach, having multiple provides much more freedom and experimentation. This is the reason for getting into machine learning and web development. Developing a deep learning model can be formed using several implementations and web development allows you to get creative with the design process. While this may seem like a strange combination of interests, both allow me to freely build something that can improve the quality of life for myself and others.
