    • Resident Assistant
    Senior Mentors are a title specific to TAMS resident assistants who are selected from the senior class. Senior Mentors are like your older brother or sister. A pair of 2 senior mentors are assigned to and live in each junior wing. We give juniors academic, social, and sometimes life advice. Aside from being the main communicator between students and admin, we also have to oversee room checks at daily curfew and wing meetings every Wednesday.
